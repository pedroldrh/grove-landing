Features

Everything your team needs to thrive

Simple tools that feel natural, so your team can focus on what matters most.

Branching Threads

Conversations grow organically. Fork discussions into branches without losing the original context. Every idea finds its path.

Harvest Reports

Automatic summaries that gather the fruits of your collaboration. Key decisions, action items, and insights — all in one place.
