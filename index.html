Our Story

A local institution for classic American fare

Since 1975, The Palms has been a cornerstone of Lexington's dining scene. Now proudly operated by 92 Proof, Inc., we continue the tradition of serving exceptional food and drinks in a warm, welcoming atmosphere that feels like home.

From hand-tossed pizzas and juicy burgers to fresh salads and daily specials, every dish is crafted with care using local and fresh ingredients. Pair your meal with our curated selection of craft beers, fine wines, and inventive cocktails.
